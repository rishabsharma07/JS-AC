Spider Man

About

Spider-Manis a superhero appearing in American comic books pubblished by Marvel Comics Created by writer editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in the comic book Amazing Fantasy 15 Aug 1962 in the Silver Age of Comics, He has been featured in comic books, tv shows, films,games,novels and plays
